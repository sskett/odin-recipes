Easy Garlic-Lemon Scallops

Description

In this simple scallop recipe, large sea scallops are saut√©ed in butter and garlic and served with a lemon-butter sauce. They will melt in your mouth!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Scallops
  • Lemon Juice
  • Seasonings

Instructions

  1. Cook the garlic in melted butter
  2. Cook the scallops on both sides until opaque, then transfer to a platter
  3. Whisk the lemon juice and seasonings into the butter, then pour over the scallops
